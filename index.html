  • Prioritize natual sources of carbohydrates
    • Rice, oats, potatoes, and fruits
  • Remove packaged carbohydrates
    • Top ramen, protein pancakes, breakfast bowls
  • Try new recipes
  • Meal prep 2-3 days of food at a time
  • Eat chicken instead of beef
    • Beef requires substaintially more resources to produce

Shredded Chicken

  1. Place chicken breasts into pressure cooker
  2. Add 1 cup of chicken broth
  3. Season with paprika, garlic powder, salt and lemon juice
  4. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes with valve sealed
  5. Vent naturally for 5 minutes
  6. Vent remaining pressure and remove chicken
  7. Shred with forks

Spinach and Fruit Smoothie

  1. Blend the following
    • 85g of spinach
    • 1 banana
    • 200g of frozen fruit
    • 24g of chia seeds
    • 56g of almonds
    • 1.5 cup of orange juice
    • 170g of greek yogurt

Protein Oatmeal

  1. Mix 1 tablespoon of chia seeds, 2 tablespoons of PB Powder and 1 scoop of protein powder into bowl
  2. Boil 1/2 cup of oats in 1 cup of water
  3. Stir consistently until oats are cooked
  4. Add oats to bowl and mix
